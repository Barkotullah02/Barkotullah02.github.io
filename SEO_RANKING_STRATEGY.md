## What Makes Knowledge Panels Appear

Google shows knowledge panels for entities meeting criteria like:

### ✅ Strong Signals
1. **Notability**: Significant coverage in reliable sources
2. **Consistency**: Same information across multiple authoritative sites
3. **Search Volume**: Many people searching your exact name
4. **Entity Recognition**: Google's algorithm identifies you as distinct entity
5. **Verified Profiles**: Blue checkmarks on social platforms help

### ❌ What Doesn't Work
- Just having schema markup (necessary but not sufficient)
- Paying for SEO services promising knowledge panels
- Creating fake Wikipedia entries (will be removed)
- Spamming name across low-quality sites

## Why This Matters More Than Just Code

- But it doesn't make people come to the restaurant
